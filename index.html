The Analog Difference

In a world of instant digital perfection, I choose to slow down. Every portrait is shot exclusively on film — Leica MP, Pentax 6x7, Rolleiflex — because some things are worth waiting for.

Film captures light differently. Skin tones breathe. Shadows have depth. There's an honesty to grain that pixels can't replicate.
